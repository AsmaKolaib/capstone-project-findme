Capstone Project Roles and Responsibilities

Role Definition

RoleRole Definition
Lead EngineerResponsible for finalising the teams and ideas (jointly with program manager). Will onbaord teams on to platfroms and set up Github respository. Also, responsible for the sprint planning, for reviewing pull requests, giving feedback on performance and retrospectives.
UX DesignerResponsible in giving critical feedback on designs and make sure the engineers understand the basics of good UX. Also enforces best practices in regard to using wireframe kits supplied to junior engineers.
Junior EngineersIn regards to the first week of the project they are responsible for submitting project ideas and voting on their peer ideas. They will then work on the project designs and integrate the UX designers feedback. With regards to the development phase, they are responsible for developing the coding infrastructure, managing their own tasks, commititng code and pull requests. Finally, they are expected to attend all project meetings, design meeting, sprint planning sessions and retrospectives on a weekly basis.
Program ManagerResponsible for making sure the junior engineers have all the documents they need, understand the phases of the capstone project and share their ideas. Will also (jointly with Lead engineers) finalise the teams and ideas.
